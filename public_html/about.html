Anton Zaytsev
Works About Resume

Anton Zaytsev is a chemist, hardware engineer, maker, and rock climber. Anton is in his last semester of a BA in Chemistry at Reed College, and pursues his passion for making and innovating through formal chemistry education and personal projects employing mechanical and hardware engineering. Anton is always open to employing his technical skills toward creating art and technology that is sustainable, creates community, and makes the world a better place.
